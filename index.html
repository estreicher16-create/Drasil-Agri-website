05 PillarsOne foundation

Every Drasil Nova app is built on the same bedrock — good infrastructure should not be rebuilt from scratch each time.

F.01 · Privacy

POPIA-compliant, by design.

South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act — baked in from day one. Your data stays in South Africa. No exceptions.

F.02 · Offline-First

The signal returns. The work catches up.

Load-shedding is a fact of life. Our apps cache everything locally and sync when connectivity returns. No half-saved forms. No lost data. Built for stage 6, 8, and everything in between.

F.03 · Nova ID

One login, every app.

Sign in once across all Drasil Nova products. No new password per app. One identity, five products.

F.04 · Pricing

Rand prices. Always.

No dollar conversions. No surprise billing when the rand moves. What you see is what you pay — in rands, via PayFast.

F.05 · Open Data

Your data is yours.

Export everything, anytime. CSV, JSON, PDF. We do not hold your data hostage. Ever.

F.06 · Roadmap

Two new stars, currently forming.

EduGate and the Worx Suite are in active development. Follow the build in public at drasilnova.co.za/roadmap.
