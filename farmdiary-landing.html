What FarmDiary does

Everything your farm needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Designed specifically for South African conditions — works offline, in Afrikaans names, with SA animal breeds.

🐄
Animal Register
Track every animal — cattle, goats, sheep, pigs and more. Tag numbers, breeds, birth dates, groups and paddocks. Full history on every animal.
🍼
Calf Register & Calving %
Log every birth with mother tag, sex and date. Automatically calculates your calving percentage and alerts you when cows have skipped calving.
🚨
Health & Sick List
Log sick and injured animals instantly from your phone. Track what's wrong, which paddock they're in, and when they recovered.
💊
Bulk Treatments
Record dipping, vaccinations and medicine with dosage and cost per animal. Select multiple animals at once. Full treatment history per animal.
👷
Worker Management
Workers clock in and out with a PIN on their own phone. Track attendance, hours worked, and who logged what on the farm.
📄
PDF & Excel Reports
Download professional reports for your bank, vet or insurance. Export full registers to Excel for your accountant. One tap — done.
🏕️
Groups & Paddocks
Organise your farm into groups and paddocks. See which animals are where at a glance. Move animals between paddocks with one tap.
📱
Works on Any Phone
No app store needed. Open in your browser and add to your home screen — works like a real app. Android and iPhone supported.
☁️
Cloud Backup
Your data is saved to the cloud automatically. Log something on your phone in the veld — it's on your PC when you get home.
